Tacos

Description

Easy taco recipe that you can make anytime.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 2 tbsp taco seasoning
  • Desired toppings
  • 1/2 cup water
  • Tortillas

Steps

  1. Cook ground beef
  2. Add taco seasoning and water. Simmer 5 min.
  3. Spoon meat mixture into tortilla. Top with desired toppings.
