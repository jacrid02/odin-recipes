Lasagna

Description

This is the best lasagna we have ever had, including restaurants! Great with salad and garlic bread

Ingredients

  • 1 package lasagna noodles
  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 3 cans tomato sauce
  • 1 can tomato paste
  • 2 tsp garlic salt
  • 5 tbsp italian seasoning
  • 1 pt ricotta cheese
  • 2 cup mozzarella

Steps

  1. Boil water and cook noodles
  2. Cook ground beef. Combine tomato sauces and paste with ground beef. Simmer. Add spices, cook for 10 min.
  3. Layer sauce, noodles, ricotta cheese, sauce, mozzarella until pan filled
  4. Bake 30 minutes at 375 degree F, let stand 15 minutes
